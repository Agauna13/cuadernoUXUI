About Me

Born in Argentina and raised in Spain, I have dedicated most of my professional life to customer service across various industries. However, my passion for technology eventually inspired me to take a bold step forward in my career. I pursued and earned the Spanish DAW degree, equivalent to an Associate's Degree in Web Development, which perfectly combines my enthusiasm for both coding and design.

Throughout my career, I've developed strong communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and a keen attention to detail, which have been fundamental in my transition to web development. My goal is to continue improving my technical skills and working on projects that allow me to create engaging and responsive web applications.

I'm proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React and Bootstrap. I am always eager to learn new tools and technologies to improve as a developer.

In my free time, I enjoy learning about new tech trends, graphic design, and working on personal web projects. I'm also passionate about cinema and photography, which allow me to approach design from a creative perspective.
